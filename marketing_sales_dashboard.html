Double Down on Q4

Invest in Corporate & VIP
Corporate segment ($10,676 avg) delivers 2.2× more per transaction than New customers. Create dedicated account management programs and exclusive B2B pricing tiers.

⚡
Fix Conversion Bottleneck
Average conversion rate is only 9.09%. A/B test landing pages, checkout flows, and email sequences. Even 1% improvement on current traffic = significant revenue lift.
↑ +$3.9M per 1% conversion rate increase
🌍
Scale Cairo Operations
Cairo generates $88.9M (25% of total) driven by transaction volume. Avg revenue ($5,933) is near mean — focus on upselling Electronics and Home Appliances in this market.

Loyalty Program Urgency
Customers with 6+ purchases have 2× higher avg revenue. Implement a points-based loyalty system with milestone rewards at 3, 6, and 10 purchases to accelerate repeat behavior.
↑ Estimated +20% customer LTV
📉
Audit Discount Strategy
Discount rate shows r = −0.022 with revenue — discounts are not moving the needle. Reduce blanket discounting. Replace with value-add offers: free shipping, bundles, early access for VIPs.
↑ Protect 2–3% gross margin immediately
